PALLETIZING

Palletizing Is The Process In Which Pallets Are Used To Stack, Store, Protect, And Transport Materials In The Course Of Being Handled By Materials Handling Equipment Such As Forklifts, Pallet Jacks Or Conveyors, Being Stored In Racking Or Bulk Storage Or Being Positioned In Transport Vehicles>

Contrary to small shipments, pallets are more secure, stable and easier to load. That's why, most of the companies and manufacturers find it a better idea to ship a pallet with bulky, large and heavy items on top, instead of fitting them into single cardboard boxes.

This Process Requires Certain Set Of Skill Along With Basic Knowledge To Handle The Freight. >
